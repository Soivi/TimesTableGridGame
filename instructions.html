Practice your multiplications! Above the grid you can see PRODUCT. Your task is to produce the correct calculation to match with the product and then place the product in the correct cell in the grid. For example, if the given PRODUCT is 8, you can place it in the convergence of cells 4 and 2, resulting then in the correct calculation. If you place the PRODUCT in the wrong cell, your phone will notify you by vibrating and you are given a time penalty. In the top right hand corner you can see your CORRECT answers and the sum of correct answers that you need to receive in order to pass the game. In the top left hand corner you can see your TIME. In the high scores section you can see your top time results.
