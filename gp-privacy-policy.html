Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the application available on Google Play, as distributing an app through Google Play requires a privacy policy. This application respects the privacy of its users.

Collection and Use of Personal Data

The application does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data. Use of the application does not require registration, and the application does not request any personal information from the user.

Device Information and Permissions

The application does not use or store any information that could identify the user. All application settings and game results are stored solely on the user's own device and are not transmitted to external servers.

Data Security

Since the application does not process personal data, there is no risk of data leakage or misuse.
